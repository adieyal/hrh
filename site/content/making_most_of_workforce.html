Training new health workers is expensive and takes time. Before committing to production of additional health workers, it is important to ensure that the existing health workforce are deployed in the right way (geographically, across different cadres), performing well, and that there are not unemployed health workers or issues with retaining existing health workers. The following questions are designed to assess whether you are making the most of existing health workers. Click on the questions below for more detail, or on the following buttons once you have made your assessment.

  • Are HRH optimally distributed and deployed?
  • Are HRH performing well?
  • Does the workforce have the right skills mix and competences?
  • Are graduates employed?
  • Do you have issues with migration?
