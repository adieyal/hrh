The Health Workforce Diagnostic Framework (HWDF) is a user-focused resource designed to help policy makers and practitioners understand and solve their problems relating to health workforce availability, accessibility, acceptability and quality. It aims to operationalise and promote the accessibility of the WHO’s Health labour market framework and policy levers for attaining universal health coverage (UHC). It also seeks to provide the ‘missing middle’ between health workforce themes and the vast array of relevant tools, resources and guidelines that exist. It provides a flexible platform that can be adapted in response to user feedback and emerging issues. Please contact Erica Wheeler (wheeler.erica@gmail.com) if you have any feedback or suggestions on how the HWDF could be improved.
